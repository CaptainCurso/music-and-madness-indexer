Music and Madness

Scanner Integration

This site belongs to a small internal tool that scans the Music and Madness Notion wiki, compares it to the catalog database, and prepares a structured work queue for indexing.

Privacy Policy Redirect Page

What This Tool Does

The scanner crawls the wiki, finds pages that are missing from the M&M Index / Catalog, flags duplicate catalog rows, and identifies orphaned rows whose source pages are no longer in scope.

Who It Is For

This site exists only to support the internal Notion integration setup for the Music and Madness project. It is not a public SaaS product or a general-purpose integration.

Integration Details

If you are filling out the Notion integration form, you can use this page for the website URL, Music and Madness Scanner Integration as the product name, and Internal scanner for the Music and Madness wiki and catalog workflow as the tagline.
